    Griham

    We aim at providing accommodation facilities to seeking students in close vicinity to their respective colleges. Moreover, the costs of these places have been sky-high but the quality of these places have rarely matched up. Griham (Sanskrit for “home”), will zoom in on each metric- be it the locality, cost or the quality of the space, and provide only the best suitable results.
